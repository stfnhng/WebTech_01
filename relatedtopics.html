Related topics of the movie Shrek

Besides the movie Shrek (2001) you can also watch, listen, play and/or enjoy other things such as movies and games. There a lot of other things so on this page you can find the highlights.

The movie Shrek 2

The second movie in the main Shrek series. This movie was released in 2004.

The movie Shrek the Third

The third movie in the main Shrek series. This movie was released in 2007.

The movie Shrek Forever After

The fourth movie in the main Shrek series. This movie was released in 2010.

The movie Puss in Boots

A spin-off movie of Shrek. The movie shows the origin story of Puss in Boots. The movie was released in 2011.

The game Shrek

A game about Shrek. The game was not very good as it received bad reviews, the gameplay and audio were bad. The game was originally released for Xbox in 2001, in 2002 there was a rework released for the GameCube called Shrek: Extra Large.

The music album from Shrek

The music used in the first movie. Especially the song All Star by Smash Mouth was a big hit. Here is a link to listen to the original album on Spotify.!!!!!!!!

The 4D simulator ride attraction

The attraction has motion-based effects and water sprayers. They are based in Japan and Singapore. Previously they were based in Orlando and Hollywood but have been closed.
